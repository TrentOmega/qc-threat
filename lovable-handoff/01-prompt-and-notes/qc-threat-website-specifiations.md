## What I want you to build

Create a polished, modern, responsive website about the quantum computing threat to Bitcoin.

The site should feel serious, technically credible, and readable for a mixed audience:

- Bitcoin beginners through advanced users
- People with little or no quantum computing background
- Readers who want both a simple overview and optional deeper technical explanations

This should not feel like a hype site or a fear-driven landing page. The tone should be grounded, editorial, and high trust. The core framing is:

"Quantum computing is a real threat to Bitcoin, but it is not game over. This is a migration race, not an overnight collapse story."

## Design direction

I want a site that feels intentional and premium, not generic startup SaaS.

- Editorial and research-led rather than marketing-led
- Clean information hierarchy
- Strong typography
- Good use of diagrams, charts, and pull quotes
- Smooth section transitions
- Clear mobile and desktop layouts
- Easy to scan, but with room for depth

Visually, it should communicate:

- technical seriousness
- clarity over hype
- Bitcoin + cryptography + future technology

Avoid:

- sensational "Bitcoin is doomed" styling
- meme-heavy crypto aesthetics
- cheesy AI visuals
- cluttered dashboards unless they genuinely help comprehension
